A self-guided Sunday walk · Lavaux, Switzerland

Lavaux Vineyard Walk—Grandvaux to St-Saphorin

Seven stops down the terraces, glass by glass, from a vine-wrapped platform to a lake-view café.

Roughly 8 km along the marked terrace paths that step down above Lake Geneva, this is a slow 6–7 hours with stops for lunch, tastings and a wander through the prettiest villages in the region. Save it for a warm Sunday and take the train home.
